About Roopa Iron Hardware

Your trusted local hardware partner in Korutla, Telangana

Our Story

Roopa Iron Hardware has been serving the Korutla community with quality hardware products and reliable service. Under the dedicated management of my uncle, we've built a reputation for fair pricing, genuine products, and personalized customer service.

What started as a small hardware shop has grown into a trusted destination for contractors, builders, and everyday customers looking for quality fasteners, tools, building materials, and fittings.

Our Mission

To provide the local community with access to quality hardware at fair prices, backed by knowledgeable service and genuine care for our customers' project success.

Why Choose Us?

  • Quality Products: We stock only reliable brands and tested materials
  • Fair Pricing: Transparent, competitive rates with no hidden costs
  • Local Expertise: Deep understanding of regional construction needs
  • Personal Service: Individual attention to each customer's requirements
  • Flexible Hours: Available when you need us, including Sundays by appointment
